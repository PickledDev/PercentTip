Help and Support

If you should find a bug or issue within any app by Pickled Development, send an email to pickleddev@gmail.com. It would be helpful to tell us what device you are using (i.e. iPhone 12), what iOS version (i.e. 14.0.1), and maybe a screen shot.

That's it. Send that information to the email address and I will see to it that your email is answered, and if possible, resolved as quick as possible.
