Information Collection and Use

I may require you to provide an email, that may or may not contain your real name. This email addresses will be used strictly for the use of communicating between you and Pickled Development. I may request what apple device you are using, what iOS version you are on, and possibly a screen shot.

Any information you send will be sent to the pickleddev@gmail.com email address. I will use any provided information to improve or correct errors within the application, or communicate through this email address for the purposes of bug collection/resolution only.

The app itself does not collect and store information on external servers. The only information stored by the app are the values for the sales tax and the percent sliders. This information is stored on the device in UserDefaults and is deleted when the app is deleted. UserDefaults is iOS's built in storage for app settings specific to the app.
